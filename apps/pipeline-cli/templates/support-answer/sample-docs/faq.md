# Frequently Asked Questions

## Is there a free plan?

Yes. The free plan includes 3 notebooks and 1 GB of storage. See the billing
guide for details on upgrading to Pro.

## Where is my data stored?

Your notes are stored securely in the cloud and synced to every device you sign
in on. Notes are encrypted in transit.

## Can I use Nimbus Notes offline?

Yes. You can read and edit notes offline; changes sync the next time you are
online.

## How do I export my notes?

Open a notebook, choose Export, and pick Markdown or PDF. Exports include all
notes in the selected notebook.

## How do I contact support?

Open Settings → Help to send a message to the support team.
