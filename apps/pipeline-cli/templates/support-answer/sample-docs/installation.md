# Installing Nimbus Notes

Nimbus Notes runs on Windows, macOS, and Linux, plus iOS and Android.

## Desktop install

Download the installer for your platform from the downloads page:

- Windows: run the `.exe` installer and follow the prompts.
- macOS: open the `.dmg` and drag Nimbus Notes to Applications.
- Linux: install the `.deb` or `.AppImage` package.

## Mobile install

Search for "Nimbus Notes" in the App Store or Google Play and tap install.

## System requirements

Nimbus Notes needs 200 MB of disk space and an internet connection for sync.
After installing, sign in with your account to download your notebooks.
