# Getting Started with Nimbus Notes

Welcome! This guide helps you get started with Nimbus Notes in a few minutes.

To get started, create a free account, then create your first note from the
"New Note" button. Getting started takes about two minutes.

Steps to get started:

1. Sign up for an account with your email.
2. Verify your email and sign in.
3. Create your first notebook, then add a note to it.
4. Start writing — your notes sync automatically across devices.

New here? The getting started checklist in the sidebar walks you through the
basics: creating notes, organizing notebooks, and sharing.
