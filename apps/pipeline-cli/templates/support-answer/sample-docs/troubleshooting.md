# Troubleshooting

Common problems and how to fix them.

## Notes are not syncing

Check your internet connection first. Then open Settings → Sync and press
"Sync now". If sync is still stuck, sign out and sign back in.

## Cannot sign in

If your password is not accepted, use "Forgot password" to reset it. Make sure
your email is verified — check your inbox for the verification message.

## App is slow or freezing

Close and reopen the app. If it stays slow, clear the local cache from
Settings → Advanced → Clear cache. Your notes are safe in the cloud.

## Still stuck?

Contact support from Settings → Help, and include the app version shown there.
