# Billing and Plans

Nimbus Notes offers a free plan and a paid Pro plan billed monthly or yearly.

## Plans

- Free: up to 3 notebooks and 1 GB of storage.
- Pro: unlimited notebooks, 100 GB of storage, and version history.

## Upgrading

To upgrade, open Settings → Billing and choose a plan. Payment is handled by
our payment provider; we accept major credit cards.

## Invoices and refunds

Download invoices from Settings → Billing → History. Pro plans are refundable
within 14 days of purchase — contact support to request a refund.

## Canceling

Cancel anytime from Settings → Billing. Your Pro features stay active until the
end of the current billing period.
